case study

Nano

Hospitals

Bannerghatta Road and Uttarahalli, Bengaluru

Objective

The main objective of the campaign is to enhance brand awareness, promote the services of Nano Hospitals, and collect leads for potential patients.

Target Audience

       Nano hospitals is basically a multispecialty hospital which provides broad range of health services including HBOT, IDD therapy and much more. Here Dr. Mohan from Orthopedic Department and Dr. Pavan Belehalli from Diabetes and Diabetic foot related issues department are very much recognized in Bengaluru. So The target audience is primarily people in Hulimavu and Uttarahalli surroundings and secondary targeted audiences are people in Bangalore and the surrounding districts.

Key Activities

  • Content Creation: Regular posts, videos, and stories highlighting services, patient testimonials, and health tips.
  • Engagement Campaigns: Interactive Q&A sessions, live events, and health awareness campaigns
  • Ad Campaigns: Sponsored posts and targeted ads to reach a broader audience.

Conclusion

The ongoing campaign for Nano Hospital has effectively enhanced its brand presence and engaged the target audience, resulting in substantial reach, engagement, and lead generation. Continued efforts and strategic planning will further consolidate the hospital's reputation and outreach in the region.
