The Sunrise Way

once upon a sunrise...

In 2017, a spark ignited. Armed with a vision and boundless passion, Mr. Sandesh M, a trailblazing entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in digital marketing, laid the foundation of Sunrise Digital Media. What started as a small team with a big dream soon evolved into a powerhouse for digital innovation.

The First Chapter

Our journey began with a bang when we collaborated with the Election Commission of India during the 2019 MP Elections in Shivamogga. Together, we made history, achieving the second-highest polling percentage in Karnataka.

From there, the momentum only grew. We proudly managed the prestigious Sahyadri Utsava and partnered with the Government of Karnataka, cementing our position as industry leaders.

Turning the Page:

Fast forward to 2022, our wings stretched further as we ventured into the UAE market and entered the dynamic world of Railway Branding. Today, our presence spans across Bengaluru (HQ), Shivamogga, and the UAE, where we work tirelessly to serve clients from diverse industries.

With 200+ clients and counting, our story is about collaboration, creativity, and delivering digital solutions that truly make a difference.

Mission:

To create meaningful, lasting impacts through media and advertising, driven by passion, creativity, and a commitment to digital excellence.

Vision:

To be the trusted digital partner that drives growth, practices ethics, and prioritizes clients, delivering solutions that empower communities and enrich lives.
